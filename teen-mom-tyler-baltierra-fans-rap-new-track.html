Teen Mom star Tyler Baltierra makes fans' 'ears bleed' as he makes rap debut in new 'cringe' track

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, June 2, 2024

TEEN Mom star Tyler Baltierra has been dragged through the mud by fans for his rap debut.

Wife Catelynn Lowell, 31, shared explicit audio from her hubby's new track. 

In the audio clip, Tyler, 31, mentioned his troubled father, Butch Baltierra, in the lyrics.

The Teen Mom OG star rapped: "F**k my sadistic granddad for beating up my dad so bad, which made him think that love is impossible to last, which made him weak and made him creep into houses, stealing any type of valuable thing, just to pawn it off. 

"Just to get some crack right back."

Catelynn captioned the post: "Keep telling him to share his art with people because it’s impactful, and people could really relate to it!"

However, in the comments section, music critics claimed their "ears were bleeding" after listening to Tyler's cringe-inducing track.

"This was very hard to listen to. I don't like it. I especially didn't like the 'huuh' thing he was doing. Very white rapper, but like he's not executing it well," one person raged.

Another added: "I got so embarrassed that I had to exit after five seconds," while a critic commented, "My ears are bleeding."

"I couldn’t listen for more than two seconds. Sorry," a user shared.

One person said: "This is awful, but good on Tyler for breaking the cycle of absentee, abusive, drug-addicted parents."

IN DEFENSE

In response to the criticism, Tyler wrote: "I'm not out here trying to be come profound musician.

"I'm just here because of the art that my pain has created."

He continued: "I never planned on sharing this stuff as it's really just been my own creative therapeutic outlet."

Tyler ended with: "But it's time to let it all go."

As the Stories continued, the Teen Mom star also thanked his wife for pushing "me to be my most authentic self.

"I'm forever grateful for her. This is one of the most hardest things I've ever had to let go of."

SEARCHING FOR BUTCH

In June, Catelynn's brother Nick Lowell shared an update on the whereabouts of Tyler's father.

In a nearly five-minute YouTube video, Nick claimed: "For all I know, he is in Texas. The last thing I heard he was doing was selling pizza.

"He was a pizza delivery guy. He might be on probation. That's the last thing I heard about him."

In June 2022, Butch was sent behind bars in Michigan to serve his sentence for allegedly Operating Under the Influence, or OUI.

Tyler and Catelynn are the parents of Novalee, seven; Vaeda, three; and youngest daughter, Rya, one.

The couple placed their firstborn, Carly, up for adoption as teens, which became a heartbreaking moment seen by 16 & Pregnant viewers.

'SO MUCH MAKE-UP'

This is not the only time fans have called out the MTV star. 

Earlier in August, during an installment from Teen Mom: The Next Chapter, Tyler grabbed an eyeliner pencil from Nova's hand and tried his best to apply it.

Viewers expressed their disapproval online and argued about how Nova was "a bit young for eyeliner."

Another upset viewer slammed: "Serious question...Why so much makeup? And why eyeliner at her age?"
