How Much Is Florence Pugh Net Worth In 2023?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, June 23, 2024

Florence Pugh Sophia Pugh has become one of the most well-known actors in Hollywood, which has increased her net worth. She made her acting start in the 2014 mystery film The Falling. Since then, she has continued to wow audiences and fans with her acting skills in a wide range of films.

She quickly became famous after getting great reviews for her role as the lead in the Shakespeare version of Lady Macbeth. After that, Pugh starred in TV versions of King Lear and Drummer Girl. The actress had a big year in 2019 when she got lead roles in Fighting With My Family, Midsommar, and Little Women.

Florence Pugh’s early life, acting roles, films, TV shows, dating history, and more will all be talked about in this piece. Keep reading to find out more.

What Is Florence Pugh Net Worth In  2023?

Celebritynetworth says that Florence Pugh is worth $8 million right now. That is a huge net worth for someone who is only 27 years old. It is thought that she makes about $2.5 million a year from brand deals and endorsements. Forbes Magazine put her on its yearly “30 under 30” list 2019. She made it into the Time Next 100 in 2021.

Florence Pugh’s Early Life

Florence Pugh was born in Oxford, England, in 1996. Clinton is her father and an owner of a restaurant. Deborah is her mother and a dancer. There are three kids in Florence’s family: Toby, Arabella, and Rafaela—both Toby and Arabella work as actresses, just like Florence.

Florence allegedly had tracheomalacia as a child, which sent her to the hospital a lot. Florence’s family moved to Sotogrande, Spain when she was only three years old so that the warm weather would help her get better. The Pugh family lived in the country for three years and then moved back to Oxford.

When it comes to school, Florence went to both Wychwood School and St. Edwards School. That being said, she wasn’t happy that these schools didn’t do much to help her reach her acting goals.

Between 2013 and 2016, the actor sang cover songs on YouTube under the name Flossie Rose.

Florence Pugh’s Movies and Series

  • The Falling (2014)

The mystery story The Falling was Florence Pugh’s first movie role. It came out in 2014. She played a teen girl at an English school for girls. Maisie Williams was her co-star. Florence was nominated for the Best British Newcomer award at the BFI London Film Festival because people liked her performance.

  • Lady Macbeth (2016)

It was in 2016 that Florence played a violent young bride in Lady Macbeth. Many critics and people in the public eye praised her work.

  • Marcella (2016)

Pugh also acted in the mystery noir show Marcella the same year. Hans Rosenfeldt, a Swedish screenwriter, wrote and directed it.

In the TV production of King Lear, which starred Anthony Hopkins, Emma Thompson, Emily Watson, and Jim Broadbent, the actor played Cordelia.

  • Outlaw King

Florence played the part of Elizabeth de Burgh in this Netflix documentary.

  • The Little Drummer Girl

It was also Pugh who played the lead role in the six-part drama The Little Drummer Girl. John le Carré’s spy book was turned into this movie.

  • Fighting With My Family (2019)

According to the 2019 movie Fighting With My Family, Florence played the part of English professional wrestler Paige. The movie was based on Max Fisher’s documentary The Wrestlers: Fighting With My Family. Jack Lowden, Lena Headey, Nick Frost, Vince Vaughn, and Dwayne Johnson were also in it.

  • Midsommar (2019)

In the same year, Florence also played the lead role in Ari Aster’s scary movie Midsommar. It was seen that the actress played a woman who joined a dangerous group after moving to Sweden with her family.

  • Little Women (2019)

Last but not least, Florence Pugh played Amy March in Greta Gerwig’s version of Louisa May Alcott’s famous book Little Women. Six Oscars were later given to the movie, and Pugh herself was nominated for Best Supporting Actress at both the Oscars and the BAFTAs.

  • Black Widow (2021)

She played the spy Yelena Boleva in the 2021 Marvel Cinematic Universe movie Black Widow. People like Scarlett Johansson, David Harbour, Olga Kurylenko, William Hurt, Rachel Weisz, and Ray Winstone have been in films with her.

  • Hawkeye (2021)

Florence played Yelena Boleva again in the Disney+ superhero show Hawkeye, where she had played the part before as Black Widow.

She then got parts in films like “Dolly,” “A Good Person,” and “Don’t Worry, Darling.”

Florence Pugh’s Awards and Nominations

  • At the BFI London Film Festival, Florence was up for Best British Newcomer for her role in the 2014 movie The Falling.
  • She got the BIFA Award for Best Performance by an Actress in a British Independent Film for her role as a violent teenage bride in Lady Macbeth, a movie based on Shakespeare’s play.
  • She was up for the BAFTA Rising Star Award at the 71st British Academy Film Awards in 2018.
  • Florence was up for Best Supporting Actress at both the Oscars and the BAFTAs for her role as Amy March in the 2019 movie Little Women.
