Josh Heupel College, Height, Age, Teams Coached, Stats

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 22, 2024

Josh Heupel is a professional football coach and former player who is currently the head coach at the University of Tennessee.

Josh Heupel college

Which college did Josh Heupel attend? Where did Josh Heupel attend college? Josh Heupel attended the University of Oklahoma.

Josh Heupel height

How tall is Josh Heupel? What is Josh Heupel’s height? Josh Heupel is 1.88 meters tall; which is 188 centimeters or 6 feet 2 inches tall.

Josh Heupel age

How old is Josh Heupel? What is Josh Heupel’s age? Josh Heupel is 44 years old.

Josh Heupel teams coached

What are some of the teams coached by Josh Heupel? Josh Heupel has coached six teams in his professional career. Some of these teams include;

  • Oklahoma (2004)
  • Arizona (2005)
  • Utah State (2015)
  • Missouri (2016-2017)
  • UCF (2018-2020)

Josh Heupel stats

What is Josh Heupel stats? As the head coach of the University of Tennessee, Josh Heupel has led the Volunteers to a bowl game and a final record of 7-6 (4-4 in conference). He also has two Steve Spurrier first year head coach awards.
