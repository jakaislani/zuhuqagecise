Biography

Rob Liefeld was born in Anaheim, CA on October 3, 1967. Comic book artist and writer most widely known for his work on Marvel’s The New Mutants and later X-Force. Then, in 1992, he would found Image Comics which would become home to his original series Youngblood. He co-founded Image Comics alongside numerous other renowned artists like Todd McFarlane and Jim Lee. A comic book lover from an early age, he began training himself to draw by tracing comics. He would later take art courses in high school and junior college.
