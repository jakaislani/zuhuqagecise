Joe Pesci biography, age, height, career, net worth

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, August 3, 2024

Who is Joe Pesci?

Joe Pesci is an actor and musician from the United States. In addition to working alongside Robert De Niro and Martin Scorsese in the movies Raging Bull, Goodfellas, Casino, and The Irishman, he is well recognized for playing tough, explosive characters in a range of genres.

Joe Pesci bio

Pesci was born in Newark, New Jersey, on February 9, 1943. His father, Angelo Pesci, worked as a forklift driver for General Motors and a bartender, while his mother, Maria (Mesce), worked part-time as a barber.

He has family ties to both Turin and Aquilonia in the province of Avellino and is of Italian ethnicity.

Joe Pesci education

Pesci attended Belleville High School and was reared in Belleville, New Jersey. Pesci began performing in plays in New York by the time he was five years old.

At the age of ten, he frequently appeared on the Startime Kids television variety program, which also starred Connie Francis.

Joe Pesci height

He is 5′ 5″ in feet and inches and 165 cm in Centimetres tall, and weighs about 154 lbs in Pounds and 70kg in Kilograms.

Joe Pesci career

Early in the 1960s, Pesci and his mother began their careers as barbers. He played guitar in various bands, including Joey Dee and the Starliters, who in New York City finally popularized the “Pepper Twist” record and dance.

He recorded his debut album in 1968, largely singing modern pop songs like “Joe Sure Can Sing!” From 1970 to 1976, he collaborated in comedy as “Vincent and Pesci” with “Frank Vincent.” They were well-known for their comedic, double-antisemitic, and insulting routines.

He made his acting debut with his comedic sidekick in the low-budget movie “Thee Death Collector.”

Following that, he was employed by an Amici’s Restaurant.

He got a lucky call from Martin Scorsese and Robert De Niro asking him to co-star as Joey LaMotta in Ragging Bull. They were the ones who expressed their admiration for him and his acting in his first movie.

Later, he played the lead in numerous independent films and won numerous prestigious accolades. Among the movies I can think of are “Dear Mr. Wonderful,” “Once Upon a Time in America,” which stars De Niro, and many others. He later worked with his comedy friend once more, and his performance in this movie was among his best. It is still remembered when he kills his companion in his wrath.

His renown and skill grew as the years went by. He also contributed to numerous blockbusters.

In 1998, he also launched his second music album, which had a flawless fusion of humorous and serious songs. He made the decision to give up acting in 1999 in order to focus on music. He soon, however, returned with his cameos.

Joe Pesci net worth

As of 2022, Joe Pesci is an American actor and musician with a total net worth of $60 million.

Joe Pesci wife and kids

Pesci has three divorces from previous marriages. A daughter was born to him from his first marriage, which took place in January 1964. His third marriage, to model and actress Claudia Haro, lasted from 1988 to 1992. Pesci and Angie Everhart split up in 2008 despite being engaged in 2007.
