Tupac's mother dead at 69

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 14, 2024

This is an archived article and the information in the article may be outdated. Please look at the time stamp on the story to see when it was last updated.

SAUSALITO, CA (KTVI)- The mother of legendary rapper/ hip-hop artist Tupac Shakur has died.

According to our partners at TMZ, Afeni Shakur Davis passed away Monday night after suffering from a heart attack at her home in Sausalito, California. She was transported to a local hospital where she was pronounced dead a short time later.

Shakur Davis was an activist and a member of The Black Panther Party for Self-Defense.

She was also the subject of ‘Dear Mama,’ a single on Tupac’s 1995 album, Me Against the World.

In the words of her late son, “Ain’t no woman alive that could take my mama’s place.”

She was 69.
