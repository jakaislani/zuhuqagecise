Michelle Madrigal finally added the picture of her and her boyfriend together on August 22, 2022. She captioned the picture “HIM.” Although, the guy’s real name has not been made public yet. 

Michelle Madrigal-Woolfolk, aka Michelle Madrigal, was born on November 14, 1971, in Quezon City, Philippines. She is a well-known actor from Filipina.

Michelle was a finalist in ABS-CBN reality talent search, Star Circle Quest. She started her career in 2007 when Michelle was 14.

Michelle Madrigal spent 12 years in her Acting career and stopped working, and she ended her career in 2016.

Michelle has two sisters, Ehra Madrigal, her elder sister, who is also an actor, and her younger sister Micaellla Madrigal. Ehra and Michelle were also seen together on the GMA network. They played some series together. 

She is one of the well-known and talented actors from the Philippines. Michelle’s net worth from the 2021 and 2022 update is around $1 Million – $5 Million. Her movie career allows her to support herself.

Michelle Madrigal’s New Boyfriend

Michelle Madrigal revealed that she had resumed dating after being divorced for about four months. They were separated for a long time, perhaps a year, but she has only been officially divorced for four months.

She shortly added “HIM” in her caption with her boyfriend. She has not officially confirmed that he is her boyfriend.

After dating for two years, they finally announced their engagement and shared their vows in 2019 in Austin, Texas.
