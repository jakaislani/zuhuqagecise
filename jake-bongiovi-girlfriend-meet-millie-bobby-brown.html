Jake Bongiovi Girlfriend: Meet Millie Bobby Brown

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, August 7, 2024

Jake Bongiovi girlfriend-American actor, Jake Bongiovi was born on May 7th, 2002 in New Jersey, United States of America.

His real name is Jacob Hurley Bongiovi and was born to the American musician, Jon Bongiovi and his wife, Dorothea.

Bongiovi shares the same parents with his three siblings; Romeo Bongiovi, Stephanie Bongiovi and Jesse Bongiovi.

He graduated from the Pennington High School and currently furthering his education at the Syracuse University in New York.

Jake Bongiovi career

Jake Bongiovi is an actor and producer per the description he gave himself on his Instagram page.

As a member of the cast of the upcoming Jordan Weiss NewLine and HBOMax film Sweethearts, Bongiovi is anticipated to make his acting debut. He is playing Kellan alongside Charlie Hall, Kieran Shipka, and Nico Hiraga in the cast.

According to reports, the project’s filming was finished in August 2022. According to reports, the filming took place in Jersey City, Cranford, and Ramapo College in Mahwah.

Alongside McKaley Miller and Teala Dunn, he was chosen to star in the upcoming Todd Tucker musical film Rockbottom, which centers on Tom Everett Scott as the primary character and fictional 1980s hair metal band CougarSnake. Justin, played by Bongiovi, has the ability to be the lead singer but first needs to get over his stage fear.

The synopsis continued, “In the present, mention of the band in an interview by Billboard-charting Gen-Z music star Bryce Cooper (Brandon Butler), whose absentee father introduced him to CougarSnake as a child, sparks a frenzy to track down the members of the group and reunite them as the opening act for Cooper’s upcoming charity concert.”

Jake will take on the role of Justin, “the prospective lead vocalist, who must conquer stage fear to lead the band back to prominence.”

Who is Jake Bongiovi’s girlfriend?

Jake Bongiovi is currently dating the British actress, Millie Bobby Brown. In March 2022, they made their red carpet debut together at the BAFTA Awards. In April 2023, the couple made their engagement official by posting separate Instagram updates.

Who is Millie Bobby Brown?

Millie Bobby Brown is a British actress and producer. She was born on February 19th, 2004 in Marbella, Spain.

Her role as Eleven in the science fiction Netflix series Stranger Things (2016–present), for which she was nominated for two Primetime Emmy Awards, brought her notoriety. 

Brown played a monster in the 2019 movies Godzilla: King of the Monsters and Godzilla vs. Kong (2021). She also produced and starred in the 2020 and 2022 installments of the Netflix mystery movie Enola Holmes.
