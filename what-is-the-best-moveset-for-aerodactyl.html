What is the best Moveset for Aerodactyl?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, April 29, 2024

What are the best moves for Aerodactyl in Pokemon GO?

Aerodactyl’s best moveset in Pokemon GO is Rock Throw and Rock Slide. These moves are both Rock-type, which aligns with Aerodactyl’s nature and gives it the STAB (Same Type Attack Bonus) effect. This increases the moves’ efficiency and deals boosted damage to enemy Pokemon.

What is the DPS of Aerodactyl’s best moveset?

Aerodactyl’s best moveset, Rock Throw and Rock Slide, has a solid DPS (Damage Per Second) rating of 12.47. This makes it a powerful choice for attacking gyms and participating in PVP battles.

Which Pokemon are the best counters for Aerodactyl in Pokemon GO?

The best counters for Aerodactyl in Pokemon GO are Primal Kyogre, Shadow Metagross, Mega Swampert, Mega Diancie, Mega Blastoise, and Shadow Rhyperior. These Pokemon have advantageous type matchups and strong movesets that can deal significant damage to Aerodactyl.

Why is Aerodactyl considered a strong Pokemon?

Aerodactyl is considered a strong Pokemon due to its high base speed of 150, which makes it the fastest unboosted Pokemon in its tier. It also has a sizable base attack of 135, and its ability Tough Claws combined with a wide range of moves like Stone Edge, Earthquake, Ice Fang, and Pursuit allow it to hit a variety of Pokemon super effectively.

Is Aerodactyl stronger than Charizard?

It can be argued that Aerodactyl is stronger than Charizard in terms of physical attacks and speed. While Charizard has stronger special attacks, Aerodactyl’s immense base 150 Speed stat makes it faster and more agile in battles.

What is the best Pokemon to beat Aerodactyl?

The strongest Pokemon to defeat Aerodactyl are Primal Kyogre, Shadow Metagross, Mega Swampert, Mega Diancie, Mega Blastoise, and Shadow Rhyperior. These Pokemon have advantageous type matchups and powerful movesets that can take down Aerodactyl efficiently.

What is the rarity of Aerodactyl in Pokemon GO?

In Pokemon GO, Aerodactyl is considered a rare Pokemon. It is said to be an ancient Pokemon that lived in prehistoric times and is only obtainable by reviving it from a fossil using the Old Amber Fossil. Catching an Aerodactyl can be a difficult task and may require some time and effort.

Can Aerodactyl be defeated by a single player in Pokemon GO?

Yes, Aerodactyl can be defeated by a single player in Pokemon GO. While it has decent defense, it is not overly challenging to defeat with a well-prepared team. With the right counters and strategy, a single player can take down Aerodactyl successfully.

Which Pokemon are faster than Aerodactyl?

Some Pokemon that are faster than Aerodactyl include Mega Aerodactyl, Deoxys (Normal Forme), Deoxys (Attack Forme), Mega Alakazam, Electrode, Pheromosa, Ninjask, and Deoxys (Speed Forme). These Pokemon have higher base speed stats and can outspeed Aerodactyl in battles.

Is Aerodactyl better than Staraptor?

While both Pokemon have their strengths, it is subjective to determine which is better. Aerodactyl has higher speed and can hit harder with its physical attacks, but Staraptor has a wider movepool and can learn powerful flying and normal-type moves. Ultimately, the choice between Aerodactyl and Staraptor depends on the player’s preferred playstyle and team composition.
