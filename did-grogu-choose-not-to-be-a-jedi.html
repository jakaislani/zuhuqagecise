Did Grogu choose not to be a Jedi?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, August 25, 2024
The Mandalorian's Grogu chooses to return to Din Djarin instead of resume Jedi training. This surprised no one – least of all Luke Skywalker.

Is Grogu not going to be a Jedi?

In the long run, it doesn't seem likely that he'll rejoin Skywalker Academy; Grogu is being set up to be the next Mandalorian Jedi, so he'll remain by Din's side for years to come. This will also allow him to dodge the lightning bolt The Senate uses to destroy the school decades later in The Rise of Kylo Ren.

Did Grogu leave the Jedi Order?

Grogu was going to be the first student that Luke ever had, and after he decided to leave, the Jedi Master likely doubted his ability to actually train a new generation of Force-users.

Why did Luke give Grogu a choice?

Luke made Grogu choose between Yoda's lightsaber and a Mandalorian chainmail shirt. The decision was meant to determine the course of Grogu's life -- as a Jedi or as a Mandalorian. At the time, Star Wars fans were appalled.

Did Grogu make the right choice?

Grogu just made the best decision of his life, and here's why. Yes, we're upset at failing to see Grogu wield a lightsaber, but we're relieved because he made the right choice. If Grogu had chosen to continue his Jedi training with Luke, it would not have been a great outcome.
