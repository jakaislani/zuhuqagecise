Who is James May?

James May whose birth name is James Daniel May was born on January 16, 1963, in Bristol, England. He grew up with his mother Kathleen, father James (an aluminum factory manager), a brother, and two sisters. May studied at Newport’s Caerleon Endowed Junior School and Rotherham’s Oakwood Comprehensive School, and he was a member of the choir at Whiston Parish Church. James later enrolled at Lancaster University’s Pendle College to study music, and he learned to play the piano and flute there. After graduation, he took a job as a records officer at a hospital in Chelsea. In 2010, Lancaster University awarded him an honorary doctorate.
