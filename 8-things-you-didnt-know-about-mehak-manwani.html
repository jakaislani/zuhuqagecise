8 Things You Didnt Know About Mehak Manwani

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, June 24, 2024

➡ Mehak Manwani Biography

A well-known face of the Indian television industry, Mehak Manwani also acted in Bollywood films and made her debut through ‘Sixteen’. Also, she has done a cameo role in the Bollywood film titled ‘Fukrey’ as Lali’s girl. Moreover, she gave an amazing performance in the popular daily soap titled ‘Sasural Genda Phool’. It seems the girl has acted in many popular television and film projects for which she got acclaimed. There is no doubt that this had made her career broad as she got huge applause from everyone. However, today we are going to share some unknown facts to know more about her personal and professional life.

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Mehak Manwani

1- Famous for

Mehak Manwani has done various television and film projects but she became popular for appearing in the most-watched television serial ‘Sasural Genda Phool’.

2- Worked with Richa Chaddha

In 2013, the stunning actress made an appearance in the Bollywood film titled ‘Fukrey’ in which she shared the screen space alongside the notable actress Richa Chaddha, Ali Fazal, and others.

3- She & Social Media

The actress is quite active on all her social media accounts where she uploads a variety of content to amuse her millions of fans and followers particularly on her Instagram account.

4- Worked with Kartik Aaryan

In 2020, Mehak Manwani also worked with the emerging, heartthrob actor, Kartik Aaryan in the Bollywood film ‘Dostana 2’ which is a sequel to the old version of the Bollywood film ‘Dostana’ starring Abhishek Bachchan, Priyanka Chopra, and John Abraham.

5- Fitness Freak

Mehak Manwani is a fitness lover as she never skips a single day for any workout and prefers gymming regularly to keep herself fit and healthy.

6- Favorite Stars

The ‘Dostana 2’ actress admires the veteran actors of Bollywood, such as; Rekha, Amitabh Bachchan, Rajnikanth, and Naseeruddin Shah for their splendid acting talent.

7- Favorite Brand/Perfume

Manwani is known for being a choosy girl when it comes to her favorite brand/perfume as she always prefers to use her favorite brands including Gucci, Lamis, Erox, and Guru Perfumes as well.

8- Love for Accessories

The actress has a love for buying a variety of accessories, such as; handbags, jewelry, gogles, hats, and clutches as well. She always prefers to buy matching stuff in which she flaunts.
