NJPW Road To The New Beginning Results (2/1): Ten-Man Tag Headlines

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

On February 1, New Japan-Pro Wrestling continued their NJPW Road to the New Beginning series with the fourth night of action from Nagano Prefectural Budokan in Saku, Nagano, Japan.

The results, courtesy of Fightful, are as follows:

  • Oleg Boltin & Shoma Kato def. Togi Makabe & Katsuya Murashima
  • CHAOS (YOSHI-HASHI & Toru Yano) & El Desperado def. House Of Torture (Yoshinobu Kanemaru, Yujiro Takahashi & SHO)
  • House Of Torture (EVIL, Dick Togo & Ren Narita) def. Tomoaki Honma, Shota Umino & YOH
  • BULLET CLUB (KENTA, Taiji Ishimori & Chase Owens) def. Guerrillas Of Destiny (Hikuleo, El Phantasmo & Jado)
  • TMDK (Kosei Fujita, Shane Haste, Mikey Nicholls & Zack Sabre Jr.) def. Hiroshi Tanahashi, Tiger Mask, Tomohiro Ishii & Ryusuke Taguchi
  • BULLET CLUB War Dogs (Alex Coughlin, David Finlay, Gabe Kidd & Clark Connors) def. United Empire (Francesco Akira, HENARE, Callum Newman & TJP)
  • Los Ingobernables de Japon (BUSHI, Shingo Takagi, Tetsuya Naito, Yota Tsuji & Hiromu Takahashi) def. Just 5 Guys (SANADA, Taichi, TAKA Michinoku, DOUKI & Yuya Uemura)
