In terms of his family history, he was raised by his single mother, Catherine, and his father’s name is unknown. Charles (brother) and Crystal (sister) are his two younger siblings (sister).

In terms of education, he received his primary and secondary education at Northwestern High School in Rock Hill, South Carolina. He then enrolled at the University of Tennessee.

Cordarrelle Patterson Height And Weight

Cordarrelle Patterson is undeniably handsome and attractive. He stands 6 feet 2 inches tall (188cm/1.88m) and weighs approximately 108 kg (238 lbs). He also has two black pairs of eyes and black hair.

Career

When it comes to his career, he began playing football in high school. He led the school team to the Class 4A Division II State Championship. Furthermore, he returned to North Carolina Tech Preparatory Christian Academy for a brief post-graduation session.

Later, he transferred to Hutchinson Community College in Kansas, where he joined the junior college football team. Patterson was an NJCAA All-American in 2010 and 2011. In his freshman year, he was also named return specialist after having 21 combined kickoff and punt returns.

During his record-breaking days at Hutchinson, his brilliance had spread across the country. Patterson enrolled at the University of Tennessee in 2012. He played wide receiver there, demonstrating his versatility as a running back and return specialist. He set the college record for most all-purpose yards (1,858) and won the Southeastern Conference championship.

Ultimately, he left college early for the NFL draft in January 2013, along with only a few other teammates. Patterson was acquired by the Minnesota Vikings in a draft-day trade with the New England Patriots in the first round of the 2013 NFL Draft.

He was named NFC Teams Player of the Month in September 2013. Patterson had 45 receptions for 469 yards and five touchdowns in his rookie season.

In addition, he signed with the Chicago Bears in March 2019. Patterson made his debut for the Bears in their season opener against the Green Bay Packers, which they lost 10-3.

In 2019, he had the most kick return yards (825) and the second-highest yards per return (29.5) in the NFL. Later, on April 15, 2021, the wide receiver agreed to terms with the Falcons on a one-year contract. He is currently playing for the team.

Cordarrelle Patterson’s Net Worth

Cordarrelle Patterson has amassed a sizable fortune during his football career. As of October 2023, his net worth is estimated to be around $5 million.

Cordarrelle Patterson Awards

Cordarrelle Patterson holds numerous NFL records. On October 27, 2013, he set a new record for the longest kickoff return touchdown: 109 yards. Similarly, he is the first player in NFL history to score a 100-yard kickoff return touchdown and a 50-yard rushing touchdown in the same season (2013).

On September 7, 2014, he set a new record for the longest rushing touchdown by a wide receiver with 67 yards. In addition, he held the record for the longest rushing touchdown by a wide receiver (67 yards).
